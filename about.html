About FilePresto

We believe file conversion should be fast, free and accessible to everyone — without compromises.

Our Mission

FilePresto was created with a simple idea: everyone should be able to convert their files quickly and for free, without installing software, creating accounts or waiting in queues.

We built a suite of 50+ tools that work entirely in your browser, meaning your files never leave your device. We believe in privacy by design.

Our Values

🆓

Free First

Core features are always free. No hidden paywalls on essential conversions.

🔒

Privacy Focused

⚡

Speed Obsessed

Every tool is optimized for speed. Conversions in seconds, not minutes.

🌍

For Everyone

Available in English, Spanish and Portuguese. Accessible to all users worldwide.
