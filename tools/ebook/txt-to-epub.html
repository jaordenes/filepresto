Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EPUB file?

EPUB (Electronic Publication) is the most widely used open standard for eBooks. It is supported by most e-readers including Kobo, Apple Books, Google Play Books, and many others.

Will my TXT formatting be preserved?

Plain text files have minimal formatting, but our converter intelligently detects paragraph breaks, chapter headings, and structure to create a well-formatted eBook.

Can I add a cover image?

Yes, the Pro version allows you to upload a custom cover image that will be embedded in the EPUB file for a professional look.

Is there a file size limit?

The Pro plan supports TXT files up to 50 MB, suitable for even the longest manuscripts and novels.
