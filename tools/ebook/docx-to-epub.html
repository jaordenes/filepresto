Frequently Asked Questions

What is a DOCX file?

DOCX is the standard format for Microsoft Word documents. It is an open XML format that stores text, images, tables, styles, and complex formatting in a single file.

Will my Word document formatting be preserved?

Yes, our converter accurately maps Word styles (Heading 1, Heading 2, etc.) to EPUB chapters, and preserves bold, italic, images, tables, and footnotes where possible.

Can I use the EPUB for publishing?

Absolutely. The generated EPUB follows the EPUB 3 standard and is compatible with major publishing platforms like Amazon KDP, Smashwords, Draft2Digital, and more.

What is the maximum file size?

Pro users can convert DOCX files up to 100 MB, supporting large manuscripts with many images and complex layouts.
