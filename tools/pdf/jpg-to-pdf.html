Frequently Asked Questions

Can I convert multiple JPG files to one PDF?

Yes, you can upload multiple JPG images and our tool will combine them all into a single PDF document. You can also reorder the images before conversion.

What image formats are supported?

Our tool supports JPG, JPEG, and also PNG files. Each image will be placed on its own page in the resulting PDF.

Will image quality be preserved?

Yes, we preserve full image resolution in the PDF output. You can also choose to optimize for web (smaller file) or print (maximum quality).

Is there a limit on the number of images?

Free users can convert up to 5 images at once. Pro users enjoy unlimited image-to-PDF conversions with no file size restrictions.
