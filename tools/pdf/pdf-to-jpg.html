Frequently Asked Questions

What resolution will the output images be?

You can choose from 72 DPI (web quality), 150 DPI (standard quality), or 300 DPI (print quality). Higher DPI produces sharper images but larger file sizes.

Can I convert specific pages only?

Yes, you can specify a page range (e.g., pages 1-5) or select individual pages to convert instead of processing the entire document.

Will text in the PDF remain readable?

At 150 DPI or higher, text remains clear and readable in the output JPG images. For documents with small text, we recommend using 300 DPI.

How do I download multiple pages?

When converting multiple pages, all images are automatically packaged in a ZIP file for easy download. You can also download individual pages separately.
