Frequently Asked Questions

Does it support XLS and XLSX files?

Yes, our converter supports both the legacy XLS format (Excel 97-2003) and the modern XLSX format (Excel 2007 and later), as well as ODS files.

Will my charts and graphs be included?

Yes, all charts, graphs, images, and visual elements in your spreadsheet will be accurately rendered in the PDF output.

Can I convert multiple sheets?

Pro users can choose to convert all sheets or specific sheets from a workbook, with each sheet appearing as a separate section in the PDF.

What if my spreadsheet is very wide?

You can use the fit-to-page option to automatically scale the content to fit within standard page widths, or choose landscape orientation for wider spreadsheets.
