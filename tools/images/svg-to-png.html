Why Convert SVG to PNG?

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is ideal for logos and icons, but not all platforms support it. PNG is universally compatible with all browsers, apps and operating systems. Converting SVG to PNG ensures your graphics display correctly everywhere.

How It Works

Our converter renders your SVG on an HTML5 Canvas at your specified dimensions and exports it as a PNG. This process is entirely local — no data is sent to any server.
