Frequently Asked Questions

What video formats are supported?

Our compressor supports all major video formats including MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, WebM, and FLV. The output is always a high-compatibility MP4 file.

How much can I reduce the file size?

Depending on the source video and quality settings, you can typically achieve 50-90% file size reduction. The actual reduction depends on the original encoding and content complexity.

Will video quality be affected?

At medium quality settings, compressed videos are visually indistinguishable from the original for most content. You can preview the result before downloading.

Is there a maximum file size?

Free users can compress videos up to 500 MB. Pro users enjoy unlimited file sizes with priority processing for faster results.
