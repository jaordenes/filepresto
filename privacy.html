Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

FilePresto is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Files you upload for conversion (temporarily stored and auto-deleted)
  • Basic usage analytics (page views, tool usage — anonymized)
  • Technical data (browser type, device type — for compatibility)

2. How We Use Your Files

Files uploaded to FilePresto are used solely for the purpose of performing the requested conversion. Files are automatically and permanently deleted within 1 hour of upload (Free plan) or 24 hours (Pro plan). We do not access, read, analyze or share your file contents.

3. Browser-Based Conversions

Many of our tools (image conversions, basic document tools) run entirely in your browser. In these cases, your files never leave your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary for the website to function properly. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics (anonymized data only) and payment processing (Pro plans). We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

FilePresto is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any data we hold about you, access information we have collected, and opt out of any non-essential data collection. Contact us at privacy@filepresto.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting a notice on our website.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@filepresto.com
