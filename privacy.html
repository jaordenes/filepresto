Privacy Policy

Your files, your privacy. Here's exactly how we handle your data.

Last updated: January 2025

🔒 TL;DR – The short version

Your files never leave your device. All conversions happen in your browser. We don't collect, upload or store your files. We may collect anonymous analytics data to improve the service.

1. Information We Collect

FilePresto collects minimal information to operate the service:

  • Anonymous usage analytics (pages visited, tools used — no personal identifiers)
  • Browser type and OS version for compatibility purposes
  • Contact form submissions (name, email, message) — only if you contact us voluntarily

We do NOT collect: your files, file contents, file names, or any personal information from your device.

2. How Your Files Are Processed

All file conversions happen entirely in your browser using technologies like WebAssembly, Canvas API, and JavaScript libraries. This means:

  • Your files are processed locally on your device
  • No file data is transmitted to our servers
  • Files are not stored, cached or logged anywhere outside your browser
  • When you close the tab, all temporary file data is discarded

3. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your language preference
  • Collect anonymous usage statistics via tools like Google Analytics
  • Improve the performance and user experience of the service

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting core functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics – anonymous traffic analysis
  • GitHub Pages – website hosting

These services may collect standard web server data such as IP addresses and browser information.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain your file data at all. Contact form submissions may be retained for up to 12 months to provide support. Anonymous analytics data may be retained indefinitely in aggregated, non-identifiable form.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any personal data you have submitted to us (e.g., via the contact form). To exercise this right, please contact us via our Contact page.

7. Children's Privacy

FilePresto is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out via our Contact page.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

FilePresto is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Files you upload for conversion (temporarily stored and auto-deleted)
  • Basic usage analytics (page views, tool usage — anonymized)
  • Technical data (browser type, device type — for compatibility)

2. How We Use Your Files

Files uploaded to FilePresto are used solely for the purpose of performing the requested conversion. Files are automatically and permanently deleted within 1 hour of upload (Free plan) or 24 hours (Pro plan). We do not access, read, analyze or share your file contents.

3. Browser-Based Conversions

Many of our tools (image conversions, basic document tools) run entirely in your browser. In these cases, your files never leave your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary for the website to function properly. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics (anonymized data only) and payment processing (Pro plans). We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

FilePresto is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

You have the right to request deletion of any data we hold about you, access information we have collected, and opt out of any non-essential data collection. Contact us at privacy@filepresto.com.
