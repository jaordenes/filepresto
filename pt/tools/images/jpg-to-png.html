Why Convert JPG to PNG?

PNG format supports lossless compression, meaning no image quality is lost during conversion. It also supports transparency (alpha channel), making it ideal for logos, icons and graphics that need a transparent background.

While JPG is great for photographs, PNG is preferred for graphics with text, sharp edges or when you need to preserve the exact original colors without any compression artifacts.

How It Works

Our converter uses the HTML5 Canvas API to read your JPG file locally in your browser and re-encode it as a PNG file. No data is ever sent to a server. The entire process happens on your device in milliseconds.
