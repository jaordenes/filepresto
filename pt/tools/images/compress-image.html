Why Compress Images?

Smaller images load faster on websites, use less bandwidth, and take up less storage space. Compressed images improve your website's performance, SEO score and user experience — especially on mobile connections.

How It Works

Our tool uses the HTML5 Canvas API to re-encode your image at your chosen quality level. Adjust the quality slider to find the perfect balance between file size and visual quality. All processing is local — nothing is uploaded.
