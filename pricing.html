Frequently Asked Questions

Is FilePresto really free?

Yes! The free plan includes access to all 50+ tools with no sign-up required. We may add ads in the future to support development, but core conversions will always be free.

When will Pro be available?

We're actively working on the Pro plan. Sign up on our contact page to be notified when it launches and receive an exclusive early-bird discount.

Are my files safe?

Absolutely. All conversions happen in your browser using WebAssembly and Canvas APIs. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

What file size limit is there?

Free users can process files up to 50MB. Pro users will be able to process files up to 500MB. Browser memory may be a limiting factor for very large files.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free plan really free?

Yes, completely. No credit card required, no hidden charges. The free plan gives you access to 100+ tools with a daily limit of 5 conversions.

How are my files handled?

Your files are encrypted in transit. On the free plan, files are permanently deleted 1 hour after conversion. Pro plan files are deleted after 24 hours.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your Pro or Business subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal. All payments are processed securely.
