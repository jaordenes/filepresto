¿Por qué convertir JPG a WebP?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior compression compared to JPG and PNG. Converting your JPG images to WebP can reduce file sizes by 25-35% while maintaining the same visual quality, resulting in faster website loading times and better user experience.

Cómo funciona

Our converter uses the HTML5 Canvas API to process your image locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Conversion happens on your device in milliseconds.
